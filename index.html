About

Morrell Electronics has been a beacon of trust and expertise, serving the Metro Detroit and larger areas since 1953. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, offering a wide range of repair services for all your electronic and vintage audio needs. From the earliest vacuum tubes to the most advanced digital circuits, we are equipped to tackle any repair challenge, ensuring that your devices are restored to their optimal performance.

Morrell Electronics services all brands of home electronic products, including stereo components, commercial audio, speakers, subwoofers, musical instruments, vintage and antique audio, and phonographs.

Open to customers from 2 to 5PM on weekdays. No appointment needed.
